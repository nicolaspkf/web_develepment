What is DAK?

DAK is ITU Copenhagen's DesignAktivitetsKomite. We are a group of students passionate about design in all its forms — from UX and UI to service design and creative coding. We create spaces to explore, learn and network across programmes and years.


Through talks, workshops and social events we bring students together around creativity and curiosity. Whether you study software development, digital innovation or something entirely different, you are welcome at DAK.
